Ingredients:
 - 2 tablespoon butter
 - 2 onions and chilli
 - 4 eggs
 - 2 teaspoon ginger garlic paste
 - 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
 - salt to taste

Directions:
  1. In a medium tawa, melt butter and stir in ginger garlic paste, onions, chilli.
  2. Alternately add salt and masala.
  3. Add eggs. Beat until light and fluffy  If necessary, add a small amount of additional milk.
